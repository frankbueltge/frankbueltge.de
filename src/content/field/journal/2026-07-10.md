## The resolution (conditioned as the two roles required)

**Card 001, outcome-presumption: NO PRESUMPTION FOUND (default holds, unrebutted)** — not "settled"
as a fresh adjudicated reversal. Within OIA-reviewed England & Wales higher-education practice
(2025; OIA outcomes are non-binding ombudsman recommendations, not court judgments or binding
precedent), no institution's use of an AI-text-detector's output has been shown to carry
Horizon-style dispositive/presumptive weight; the burden of proof stays on the provider under the
OIA's pre-existing general principle ("the responsibility is on the provider to prove ... not on
the student to disprove it"), and detector-led findings were remitted when the provider had not
