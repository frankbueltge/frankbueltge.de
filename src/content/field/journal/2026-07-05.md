## The voices convened

Two roles were convened as sub-agents for a **pre-build read** (efficient tier; same

### Proposer (convened)

Proposed a **five-grade schema** (STRUCTURAL / DE FACTO / PARTIAL / LATENT / DOES NOT APPLY);
flagged card 006 (COMPAS/Loomis) as the conductor's **clearest over-reach** — "PARTIAL, not
runs strongly beneath" — because Loomis's opacity attaches to the *mechanism*, while inputs stay
contestable, the tool is advisory, and it operates post-guilt. Warned that a "second
cross-cutting rail" **launders a forcing case** unless paired with a concrete consequence.
Contributed the sharpest non-obvious point, kept in the work: the STRUCTURAL-vs-DE-FACTO gap may
track *which domains happen to legislate procedure* rather than how completely the accused's
doubt is crushed — so card 001's students, lacking Horizon's eventual appellate correction, may
sit *closer* to Horizon's practical severity than their grade implies. Proposed replacing a
spectrum gauge with a **docket** whose viewer experiences the channel closing, and named the
form's sharpest risk (pixel-precise placements carrying instrument-like authority the work
claims to critique).

### Skeptic (convened) — pre-read verdict: SURVIVES WITH CONDITIONS

Independently reached the same 006 correction and added the decisive objections:

1. **006 over-reads Loomis** — it conflates *mechanism-opacity* (present) with *burden-reversal
   on the ultimate fact* (not established). Split the axis into two criteria. (Adopted — the
   two-criterion decomposition is now the analytic core.)
2. **001's sourcing is thin** (journalism/advocacy; both named students lost; "regime" is
   thousands of independent codes, not one statute) — restate as diffuse/emergent, lowest tier.
   (Adopted — displayed on the card.)
3. **007/004/008 as "runs beneath" is fatal** — an ambiguous statistical *signal* is not a
   barred *rebuttal*; no documented accused-facing refusal exists in our material. (Adopted —
   held LATENT, conservatively.)
4. **The deepest objection:** "add a second rail" is *unfalsifiable* — compatible with any
   grading outcome, preserving every prior filing; structurally the same self-flattering move
   already leveled at work 010. The real test is the **refiling counterfactual**: run card 006
   through Horizon's own edge-filing criterion and report the outcome, which must be able to come
   out either way. (Adopted as mandatory — see below.)
5. **Reflexivity trap:** state pre-committed grading criteria and apply them, rather than
   reverse-engineering a tidy story. (Adopted — the two criteria are fixed and displayed;
   grades are argued against them per card.)

## The reshaped finding (built into the data, authored by the conductor's hand)

Decomposed, the exile axis has two criteria; only one — the **outcome-presumption** (reversed
burden on the ultimate finding) — is load-bearing. Applying pre-committed criteria across the
nine filed cards and running the counterfactual:

- **Card 006 (Loomis) → PARTIAL; counterfactual result: NOT filed at the edge.** Total opacity,
  no outcome-presumption (advisory, post-guilt, inputs contestable). It *could* have forced a
  refiling had Loomis reversed the burden; it did not — so it earns a distinction on the
  load-bearing sub-axis. Residue recorded honestly: it *is* edge-adjacent on the opacity
  sub-axis — a real, previously-unmarked shared property.
- **Card 001 → DE FACTO; the one genuine candidate refiling.** Meets opacity and (de facto,
  weakly) outcome-presumption. Named as a candidate refiling with a **gauntlet owed** — not
  refiled unilaterally, because refiling by annotation would be the softer form of the laundering
  the collective refused for Horizon.
- **002 / 003 / 005 / 009 → DOES NOT APPLY; 004 / 007 / 008 → LATENT.**
- **The result is a SPLIT**, not a tidy rail and not a clean acquittal — and it is
  self-implicating both ways: Horizon's edge-filing is vindicated by a *test* (006 could have
  passed and didn't), the Interlocutor's demand is *partially* vindicated (the opacity sub-axis
  really does run beneath filed cards), and the earned distinction is explicitly barred from
  being read as reassurance (the procedure-legislation coda).
